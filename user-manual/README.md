# AuraGrade User Manual

Welcome to AuraGrade! This guide will help you understand and use every feature, step by step, even if you are a complete beginner. Each section explains what you are doing, why it matters, and how to do it—like teaching a kid!

---

## 1. Opening AuraGrade

**What:** Start the AuraGrade app in your web browser.

**Why:** This is where you will grade answer scripts, set up exams, and manage everything.

**How:**
- Open your web browser (like Chrome or Edge).
- Type `http://localhost:3000` in the address bar and press Enter.
- You will see the AuraGrade landing page.

---

## 2. Logging In (Staff)

**What:** Log in as a staff member to access grading and admin features.

**Why:** Only staff can grade scripts and manage exams.

**How:**
- Click the **Staff Login** button at the top right.
- Enter your email or use Google login.
- Follow the instructions to complete login.

---

## 3. Accessing the Grading Dashboard

**What:** Go to the dashboard where you can upload and grade answer scripts.

**Why:** This is where grading happens.

**How:**
- On the landing page, click the **Staff Grading** button.
- You will be taken to `/grading`.

---

## 4. Selecting an Assessment

**What:** Choose the exam or test you want to grade.

**Why:** Grading is linked to a specific assessment.

**How:**
- In the grading dashboard, find the **Assessment** dropdown.
- Click it and select the assessment you want.

---

## 5. Selecting a Student

**What:** Choose the student whose answer script you are grading.

**Why:** Grades are saved for each student.

**How:**
- Find the **Student** dropdown or input.
- Select or type the student’s name or registration number.

---

## 6. Uploading an Answer Script

**What:** Upload a scanned image of the student’s handwritten answer sheet.

**Why:** The AI needs the image to grade.

**How:**
- Click the upload area or button (usually says "Upload" or has a paperclip/image icon).
- Select the image file from your computer.
- Wait for the preview to appear.

---

## 7. Starting Grading

**What:** Begin the grading process.

**Why:** The AI will read and grade the answer sheet.

**How:**
- Click the **Start Grading** button.
- Wait for the grading to finish.
- You will see feedback, score, and confidence.

---

## 8. Saving Results

**What:** Save the grade to the database.

**Why:** So records are kept and can be reviewed later.

**How:**
- Click the **Save** or **Approve** button if available.
- The grade is now stored.

---

## 9. Handling Appeals or Flags

**What:** Manage scripts flagged for review or appeals.

**Why:** Some scripts may need extra attention.

**How:**
- Follow on-screen instructions for flagged scripts.
- Use the **Appeal** or **Audit** features as needed.

---

## 10. Logging Out

**What:** Sign out when you are done.

**Why:** Keeps your account secure.

**How:**
- Click your profile or the logout button.
- Confirm logout.

---

## Tips for Beginners
- Read every button and label carefully.
- If you get stuck, look for help or tooltips in the app.
- Don’t be afraid to ask questions or try things!

---

## Troubleshooting
- If something doesn’t work, refresh the page.
- Make sure you are logged in as staff.
- Check your internet connection.
- Ask for help if needed!

---

This manual will grow as new features are added. If you need more details or step-by-step guides for any operation, just ask!
